# CodeAlpha Robotics and Automation Internship

## Project Overview

This repository contains the projects and tasks completed during the CodeAlpha Robotics and Automation Internship. The internship provides practical exposure to robotics concepts, automation systems, robotic programming, sensors, and intelligent control mechanisms.

The main objective of this internship is to understand the fundamentals of robotics, automation technologies, and the development of robotic systems that can perform tasks efficiently with minimal human intervention.

## About CodeAlpha Internship

CodeAlpha is a software development company focused on innovation and emerging technologies. The Robotics and Automation Internship provides students with hands-on experience in designing, programming, and understanding automated systems.

During this internship, various concepts related to robotics, automation, sensors, actuators, control systems, and robotic simulations were explored.
